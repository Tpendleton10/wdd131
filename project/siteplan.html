Site Plan: Las Vegas Fire and Rescue

Site Name

Las Vegas Fire and Rescue — This name represents the official emergency and public safety department serving the city of Las Vegas, Nevada. The name is both functional and immediately recognizable, ensuring trust and clarity.

Site Purpose

The site will serve as an informational hub for the Las Vegas Fire and Rescue department. It will provide emergency contact information, describe fire safety services and programs, and offer community education tips on fire prevention and emergency readiness.

Scenarios

  • How can I request a fire inspection for my business or home?
  • Where can I find safety tips for creating a fire escape plan?

Color Scheme

  • Red (#C8102E) – Primary color used in headers, navigation bar, and buttons to reflect urgency and fire department identity.
  • Yellow (#FFC72C) – Used as an accent color for icons, call-to-action elements, and highlights for safety info.

Typography

  • Roboto – Used for body text for readability.
  • Oswald – Used for headings and titles to create a bold, official look.
